EXT. FOREST - DUSK

BLANK SCREEN turns to taped video static. Video turns to low quality hi-8 video style. Unknown character is shown.

JOHN

Alright so I've hit this button here and then I just hit this one right? YES! It works. So um. Hi I'm John. I go to Oak Park University and this is my final year history assignment. I'll be covering the the forest that sits a few kilometers from the campus. Now this forest is known for some crazy shit. There's been murders, disappearances. Some people have been known to hallucinate but they were all nutcases.


John flips the camera around to show the entrance to a forest of redwoods. (This would be something like the Redwood Forest in East Warburton) He moves further into the forest while explaining about the history.

JOHN

You're probably wondering about the murders, and well, they're all unsolved. Same with the people who went missing. But, there are suspicions about some old Oak Park University students who lost it, ran into the forest and haven't left since. It would make sense though. Back in 1964, some students went missing. A year later, a body turned up belonging to one of the missing students. Missing limbs and seemingly ripped open, as if eaten. Forensics experts found fingerprints and DNA from some of the other students which led some to believe they'd turned into a cannibal cult.

John stops walking and flips the camera back around to show his face

JOHN

You ask me? I think it's a load of crap.

As John finishes his sentence snapping of branches and crunching of leaves can be heard.

JOHN

Did you hear that? Must be some fox or something. I'll go have a look. I'll leave you here. If anyone finds this. Something probably happened to me. So, I'd run back to your car or back to the town.

John places the camera down on a rock and walks away

JOHN (SHOUTING)

And look how tall these trees are!

John steps behind a tree before the tape glitches out and ends.


EXT. FOREST ROADSIDE - NIGHT

The camera comes back to life and starts recording as a new person starts the camera.

ELIOT

God what on earth happened to this thing. It's all scratched up. It's so... old.

Eliot messes around with the camera trying to figure out how it works. Feeling as though it's not worth trying to get to work he begins to try and hitchhike. A car drives past and splashes water right into Eliot.

ELIOT (SHOUTING)

Are you serious right now. Come on mate. You had the entire bloody road. Couldn't you at least not hit the puddle. (Mutters curses)

Time passes and eventually a van pulls over to pick Eliot up. The back door opens up and a man grabs onto Eliot and forces them into the van. Eliot fights back. Screaming for help. They fall to the ground before getting knocked out and dragged into the van the camera sits on the side of the road before glitching again freeze framing on the van door open.
